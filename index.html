About Mathew Kwabena Boakye
Chief Executive Officer

" Every big idea starts with a name — and for KB Ventures, that name is Matthew Kwabena Boakye. If you ask me, this isn’t just business — it’s personal. Every service under KB Ventures came from a real-life challenge I faced or someone close to me did. Travel struggles. Limited trade access. The hustle of grooming. The dream of owning property. I didn’t just find the answers — I built the solutions. Today, I see myself as a lifestyle builder — not someone chasing trends, but someone solving problems that hold people back from living better. I started KB Ventures not just to make sales, but to bridge worlds: The local to the global. The everyday to the extraordinary. My story? Not some overnight miracle. It’s grit, mistakes, lessons, and relentless belief in what’s possible. And now, I’m opening that door to others — because I know how it feels to stand outside it. “I didn’t just want to sell services — I wanted to give people options.”
